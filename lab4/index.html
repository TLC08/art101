Drawing a chair:
1. Collect your materials
	a. Sketch book, pens, pencils, erasers
2. Find your subject chair (can be any chair)
3. Position the chair in any way you like
	a. Sunlight or artificial light can be hitting it, can be placed at an angle
4. Observe the chair
	a. Pay attention to the chair's design - the curves, the surface, and how much detail certain parts of the chair has
5. Create an outline sketch of the chair
	a. Doesn't have to be detailed, jot down main features, like the legs, base, and back
6. Shade in your sketch
	a. Lightly shade the parts where the light hits the chair, but shade the shadows darker
7. Trace the outline of the chair with a pen (any pen)
8. You are done!
